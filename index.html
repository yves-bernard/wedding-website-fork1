How do I get there?

From Ireland:

Pretty much three options: 1. Take a plane to toulouse airport. Aerlingus and Ryanair have frequent flights, and this is the most convenient, as Toulouse airport is only 20min from the city center and about 50min from the wedding venue. 2. Take a plane to Carcassone airport. This is a good bit further but have frequent ryanair flights. It's a about 1 hour driving from the wedding venue. 3. Take a boat to Bilbao in spain en drive for about 4 hours. You'll drive through very beautiful landscape.
