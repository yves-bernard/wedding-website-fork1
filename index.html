TOULOUSE

How do I get there?

From Ireland:


Pretty much three options:

1. Take a plane to toulouse airport. Aerlingus and Ryanair have frequent flights, and this is the most convenient,
Toulouse airport is only 20min from the city center and about 50min from the wedding venue.

2. Take a plane to Carcassone airport. This is a good bit further but have frequent ryanair flights. It's a about 1 hour driving from the wedding venue.
It's a good option if you're thinking to rent a car

3. Take a boat to Bilbao in spain en drive for about 4 hours. You'll drive through very beautiful landscape.

From France:

You'll manage.

Where to stay?


It really depends on when you arrive and what you want to do.
We suggest sleeping near the wedding venue on Friday and Saturday evenings for convenience.
If you have time, it's well worth spending a night in Toulouse and exploring the city (check out the list of ‘Things to do in Toulouse’ below).

Where to sleep in Toulouse:

 We recommend staying in the city centre to get the best experience of Toulouse. You will find lots of accommodation on Booking.com and AirBnB, and we’re happy to advise if you have any questions. You won’t go far wrong if you stay in these areas: Les Carmes, Saint-Etienne, Capitole, Saint Sernin, Saint-Cyprien (our neighbourhood!), Saint Georges, Saint Aubin, Dupuy.
All these areas are very central, near nice restaurants,and well serviced with transport from the airport.
The city can be explored on foot or using the VeloToulouse city bike rental scheme.

How to go to the wedding ?


The most convenient is to rent a car (or take the ferry).
The venue is about 35min driving from Toulouse or 1h15 from Carcassone
This will also allow you to explore some of the gems in the area if you have a bit of extra time (See to do in the area below)

If you prefer not to drive, then the other option is going to be taxis and Uber / Bolt.
If this is your chosen mode of transportation, please let us know with your plane arrival and address of stay, and
we will organise the taxis pick-up to/from wedding/airport/toulouse for you (as most drivers will be French speakers).

Car Pooling: If you pick taxis, we'll try to do car-sharing ; also some french drivers may have some seats so we let you know !

What to do in the area:


Easiest is to rent a car ; driving 35-40min (road is very good, no worries).

If you prefer not to rent a car, the other options is going to be taxis or Uber/Bolt. Unfortunately, public transport is not great. From toulouse, it will be easy to find a taxi. Provide link to a few companies and ask for quotes From near the wedding venue, these companies can provide taxi services. We can help you with organising taxis if needed.

Car Pooling: If you have spare room in your car, it would be great to carpool when possible.
